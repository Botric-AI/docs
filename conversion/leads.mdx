The Leads page shows all contacts captured by your AI agents. When a visitor interacts with your [Chat Agent](/engagement/chat-agent/overview) and provides their contact information, Botric automatically creates a lead record with their details and conversation history.

{/* IMAGE: Leads table view showing captured leads */}

## How Leads Are Captured

Leads are created automatically when a chat visitor provides their contact information during a conversation. The typical flow:

1. A visitor opens your chat widget and starts a conversation.
2. The Chat Agent engages the visitor based on its purpose (support, lead gen, sales, etc.).
3. The visitor provides their name, email, or other contact details.
4. Botric creates a lead record and stores the conversation history.

<Note>
  Lead capture depends on your bot's purpose and prompts. Bots configured for **Lead Generation** or **Sales** are optimized to collect visitor information naturally during conversations.
</Note>

## Leads Table

The leads table provides a comprehensive view of all captured leads with search, filtering, and pagination.

### Columns

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | The visitor's name (if provided) |
| **Email** | The visitor's email address |
| **Source** | Which bot or agent captured the lead |
| **Date** | When the lead was captured |
| **Status** | Current lead status |

### Search and Filter

- **Search** — Find leads by name, email, or any captured field.
- **Filter by source** — Show leads from a specific bot or agent.
- **Filter by date** — Narrow results to a specific time range.
- **Pagination** — Navigate through large lead lists with page controls.

{/* IMAGE: Leads table with search and filter controls */}

## Lead Details

Click on any lead to open its detail view. The lead detail page shows:

### Contact Information

All form data captured during the conversation, including:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number (if provided)
- Company name (if provided)
- Any custom fields collected by the bot

### Conversation History

The full chat transcript between the visitor and your bot. This gives you context about what the lead was interested in, what questions they asked, and how the conversation went.

{/* IMAGE: Lead detail view showing contact info and conversation */}

<Tip>
  Review conversation histories before following up with leads. Understanding what a lead discussed with your bot helps you personalize your outreach.
</Tip>

## Exporting Leads

Export your leads for use in external CRM systems, email marketing tools, or spreadsheets.

- Click **Export** to download your leads as a CSV file.
- Use filters before exporting to narrow the export to specific leads.
