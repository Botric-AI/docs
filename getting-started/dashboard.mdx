The dashboard is your command center in Botric. It gives you a high-level view of your website's health, AI search visibility, engagement metrics, and a guided checklist to help you get the most out of the platform.

{/* IMAGE: Full dashboard view showing all cards */}

## Dashboard Cards

Your dashboard is organized into cards, each highlighting a key metric or insight.

### Leads Trend

Displays the number of leads captured over time. The chart shows daily or weekly lead volume so you can spot trends and measure the impact of your AI agents.

- Click the card to go to the full [Leads](/conversion/leads) view.

{/* IMAGE: Leads Trend card with chart */}

### AI Health

Shows the overall health and performance of your AI agents. This includes response quality indicators, conversation volume, and agent activity status.

- Quickly see which agents are active and how they're performing.

{/* IMAGE: AI Health card */}

### Site Health

Displays your latest [Site Audit](/discovery/site-audit) score with a letter grade (A through F). The score reflects your website's technical health, content quality, and performance.

| Grade | Score Range | Color |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| A | 90–100 | Blue |
| B | 80–89 | Green |
| C | 70–79 | Orange |
| D–F | Below 70 | Red |

- Click the card to view the full audit report.

{/* IMAGE: Site Health card showing grade */}

### GEO Visibility

Shows your Generative Engine Optimization score — how visible your website is across AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini.

- Click to explore the full [GEO Agent](/discovery/geo-agent/overview) dashboard.

{/* IMAGE: GEO Visibility card */}

### SEO Overview

Provides a snapshot of your traditional search engine optimization metrics.

<Note>
  The full SEO Agent is coming soon. Currently the dashboard shows a preview of available metrics.
</Note>

{/* IMAGE: SEO Overview card */}

## Get Started Checklist

The dashboard includes a **Get Started** checklist that guides you through key setup steps. Each item links to the relevant feature:

- Complete onboarding
- Run your first site audit
- Create a Chat Agent
- Add a knowledge source
- Deploy your chatbot
- Connect an email mailbox

As you complete each step, it's marked as done on the checklist.

{/* IMAGE: Get Started checklist card */}

<Tip>
  Complete all checklist items to get the most value from Botric. Each step unlocks additional features and insights.
</Tip>

## Workspace Switcher

If you manage multiple websites, use the workspace switcher in the sidebar to switch between workspaces. Each workspace has its own dashboard, agents, and settings.
