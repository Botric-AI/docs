The Plans & Billing page lets you view your current plan, manage your subscription, and access billing history.

<Snippet file="coming-soon-badge.mdx" />

## What to Expect

### Plans

View and compare available Botric plans. Each plan includes different limits for:

- Number of workspaces
- AI agent conversations per month
- Knowledge source storage
- Number of admin users
- Access to advanced features (GEO Agent, Email Agent, etc.)

### Subscription Management

Manage your active subscription:

- Upgrade or downgrade your plan
- Update payment method
- View your current billing cycle
- Cancel or pause your subscription

### Invoices

Access your billing history and download invoices:

- View past invoices with dates and amounts
- Download invoices as PDF for your records
- Track payment status

## Current Access

While the billing system is being finalized, you can continue using Botric with your current plan. Contact [support@botric.ai](mailto:support@botric.ai) for any billing questions or plan changes.
