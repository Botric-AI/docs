The SEO Agent provides AI-powered insights into your website's search engine optimization performance, helping you improve your organic search rankings and drive more traffic.

<Snippet file="coming-soon-badge.mdx" />

## What to Expect

The SEO Agent will offer:

- **Keyword Tracking** — Monitor your rankings for target keywords across search engines.
- **On-Page Analysis** — Get recommendations for improving meta tags, headings, content structure, and internal linking.
- **Backlink Monitoring** — Track your backlink profile and identify new linking opportunities.
- **SERP Analysis** — See how your pages appear in search results and optimize your snippets.
- **Actionable Recommendations** — Receive AI-generated suggestions tailored to your website and industry.
