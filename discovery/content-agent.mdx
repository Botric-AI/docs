The Content Agent helps you create, optimize, and manage website content using AI — ensuring your pages are well-written, relevant, and optimized for both traditional and AI-powered search engines.

<Snippet file="coming-soon-badge.mdx" />

## What to Expect

The Content Agent will offer:

- **Content Generation** — Create blog posts, landing pages, and product descriptions powered by AI.
- **Content Optimization** — Get suggestions to improve existing content for readability, SEO, and AI search visibility.
- **Tone and Brand Alignment** — Ensure all generated content matches your brand voice and style guidelines.
- **Content Calendar** — Plan and schedule content creation with AI-assisted topic suggestions.
- **Performance Tracking** — Monitor how your content performs across search engines and AI platforms.
