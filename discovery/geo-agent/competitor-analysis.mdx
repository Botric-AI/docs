The Competitor Analysis feature lets you see how your brand's AI search visibility compares to your competitors. Add competitor domains and Botric runs the same GEO analysis, giving you a side-by-side view of where you lead and where you're falling behind.

{/* IMAGE: Competitor analysis comparison dashboard */}

## Adding Competitors

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Competitor Analysis">
    Navigate to **Discovery > GEO Agent > Competitor Analysis** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a competitor">
    Click **Add Competitor** and enter the competitor's domain (e.g., `competitor.com`). You can add multiple competitors to compare against.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run analysis">
    Botric runs the same set of queries against each competitor's brand and generates comparison data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Competitor analysis uses the same query set as your own GEO analysis, so comparisons are apples-to-apples.
</Note>

## Side-by-Side Comparison

The comparison view shows:

- **Overall visibility score** for each brand
- **Engine-by-engine breakdown** — how each competitor performs on ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and other AI engines
- **Intent comparison** — which intent types each brand dominates
- **Query overlap** — queries where multiple brands appear together

{/* IMAGE: Side-by-side competitor comparison chart */}

### Visibility Score Comparison

A bar or radar chart shows your visibility score alongside each competitor across all tracked AI engines. This gives you a quick view of your competitive position.

{/* IMAGE: Radar chart comparing visibility scores */}

### Query-Level Comparison

Drill down into individual queries to see exactly which brands appear for each one. This helps you identify:

- **Queries you own** — where only your brand appears
- **Contested queries** — where multiple brands compete for visibility
- **Missed opportunities** — queries where competitors appear but you don't

{/* IMAGE: Query-level comparison table */}

## Using Competitor Data

<Accordion title="Identify content gaps">
  If a competitor appears for queries where you don't, examine their content strategy. What pages, blog posts, or resources are they publishing that earn them AI citations?
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Strengthen your positioning">
  For contested queries, improve your content depth and relevance to increase your chances of being cited over competitors.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Track competitive shifts">
  Monitor the comparison over time to see if your improvements are narrowing the gap or if competitors are gaining ground.
</Accordion>
