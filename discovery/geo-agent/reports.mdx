GEO Reports give you a comprehensive view of your AI search visibility in a structured, shareable format. Use reports to track progress over time, share results with your team, and document your GEO strategy.

{/* IMAGE: GEO report overview page */}

## Generating a Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Reports">
    Go to **Discovery > GEO Agent > Reports** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the report">
    Select the time period and scope for your report. You can include:
    - Overall visibility scores
    - Engine-by-engine breakdowns
    - Intent analysis summaries
    - Competitor comparisons (if configured)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate Report**. Botric compiles your data into a structured report.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reading a Report

Each report includes the following sections:

### Executive Summary

A high-level overview of your GEO performance including:

- Overall visibility score and trend direction
- Top-performing AI engines
- Key changes since the last report

### Visibility by Engine

Detailed scores and query counts for each AI search engine:

- **ChatGPT** — OpenAI's conversational AI
- **Perplexity** — AI-powered research assistant
- **Gemini** — Google's AI search experience
- Other tracked engines

### Intent Breakdown

A summary of how your brand appears across different [intent types](/discovery/geo-agent/intent-analysis) — informational, commercial, transactional, and navigational.

### Competitor Comparison

If you've added competitors, the report includes a side-by-side comparison of visibility scores and query coverage. See [Competitor Analysis](/discovery/geo-agent/competitor-analysis) for more details.

### Recommendations

AI-generated suggestions for improving your GEO visibility based on the analysis findings.

## Exporting Reports

You can export reports for sharing or record-keeping:

- **PDF** — A formatted document suitable for presentations and stakeholder reviews.
- **CSV** — Raw data export for custom analysis in spreadsheet tools.

Click the **Export** button at the top of any report and select your preferred format.

<Tip>
  Generate reports on a regular schedule (weekly or monthly) to track your progress and spot trends over time.
</Tip>

## Report History

All generated reports are saved and accessible from the Reports page. You can:

- View any past report
- Compare reports from different time periods
- Track how your visibility score has changed over time
