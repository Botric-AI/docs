## What is Botric?

Botric is an AI-powered platform built around three core pillars:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Discovery" icon="magnifying-glass-chart" href="/discovery/site-audit">
    Audit your website's health, analyze your visibility across AI search engines, and uncover opportunities to outperform competitors.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Engagement" icon="comments" href="/engagement/chat-agent/overview">
    Deploy AI-powered chat and email agents that interact with your visitors and customers using your own knowledge base.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Conversion" icon="chart-line-up" href="/conversion/leads">
    Capture leads, manage support tickets, log calls, and track every interaction to turn engagement into revenue.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [app.botric.ai](https://app.botric.ai) using your email or Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Walk through the 5-step onboarding wizard to set up your workspace, connect your website, and configure your brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    View your site health, AI search visibility, and engagement metrics at a glance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy an AI agent">
    Create a Chat Agent or Email Agent powered by your knowledge sources and start engaging customers.
  </Step>
</Steps>
